Miscellanea

    Complex function plotter – A place to graph functions on the complex plane! Click and drag to move around, press the - and + keys to zoom. Here are some examples:
    • \(\begin{align} \frac{1}{x} \ln(x-1)^2 \end{align}\)
    • \(\begin{align} \prod_{n=1}^4\left( (x/n)^{-5} - i^n\right) \end{align}\)
    • \(\begin{align} \sin\left(\ln\left( e^{x^2} \right)\right) \sin\left(\ln\left( e^{x^3-\pi/2} \right)\right) \end{align}\)
    • \(\begin{align} \frac{x^2}{(x-\zeta_1)(x-\zeta_2)(x-\zeta_3)} \end{align}\) , where the \(\zeta_i\) are the third roots of unity
    • \( \mathrm{tet}(\sin(x),10) \) , where \(\mathrm{tet}\) is tetration — \(\mathrm{tet}(x, n) = \left. x^{x^{...^{x}}} \right\} n\ x\text{'s} \).
    Comps – An overview of some cool research I did on newly reducible polynomials.
    Pentris – It's like tetris, but with pentominos and reflection!

    Controls:

    Left / Right – Move piece sideways
    Up – Rotate piece
    X – Reflect piece
    C – Store piece for later
    Down – Soft drop
    Space – Hard drop

    Mandelbrot Set Explorer – A program I wrote a few years ago to zoom around the Mandelbrot set.
